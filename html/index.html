A Byte About Us

Alphabit, GBPEC Delhi's programming club, was founded in 2017. Alphabit is a community of students highly enthusiastic about Coding, Web development, Android development and all other aspects of programming.The aim of Alphabit is to promote programming as a culture in the Institute. The club brings people together, so that they may hone their skills in programming.

We aim to prepare ourselves better for programming competitions like ACM-ICPC, and keep our community aware about upcoming events and help students be ready for their placements. Apart from this, we also develop various applications to be used by the campus junta and promote open source development.

Bottom line, if you are @ GBPEC Delhi and you think you can fall in love with coding, Alphabit is the place for you!
